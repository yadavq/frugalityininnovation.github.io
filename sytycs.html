GIIEC HBTU had organized a live event- So You Think You Can Solve which aimed to let the aspiring students use their social media platforms so that they bring forward their innovation and ideas and at the same time using the most common pathway, these social networking sites by giving brainstorming solutions to the provided situations.

The main guidelines of the event were-

  1. We provided five situations based on startups: The candidates have to choose any one of the situations and submit the solution on our Facebook page Gen-x Innovation Incubation and Entrepreneurship Cell or mail it to us.
  2. Then, we published the solutions on our social media platforms.
  3. The solution getting the most likes qualified for the final round.
  4. The top 3 solutions would be recognized on all social media handles of E-Cell and the prize money available was worth RS 1000.
