B I Z   Q U I Z
"Great things in business are never done in person . They are done by a team of people. " -Steve Jobs

A quiz event which deals with company histories, business strategies, well known deals, people in news, acquisitions and future business developments. Difficulty and types of questions depends on the quizmaster.

Rules and Guidelines:
1. Only team entries are allowed
2. The team shall consist of two persons only.
3. Cross college teams are not allowed.
4. Give yourself a good team name.
5. The participants are not allowed to use any electronic gadgets or else they will be disqualified.
6. The decision of Quizmaster will be final.
7. The questions shall be in the form of MCQ,true/false or specific one word answer.
8. Audience shall not give any hints or discuss among themselves.
9. Replacing any team member is not allowed after registration.
10. Registration is to be done till 19th April, 2019 on our website.
11. Only team leader will register with his/her team member.

Round 1:
Venue: Auditorium
Date: 20 April,2019.
1. A minimum of 20 questions will be asked.
2. This round will be pen-paper based.
3. Each correct answer gains you +1 points and there is no negative marking.
4. 5 questions are (*) marked which will be deciding factor in case of ties.
5. Time limit: 30 minutes.
6. Top 6 teams will be selected for round 2.

Round 2:
Venue: Incubation Hub
Date: 21 April,2019.
1. The structure of final round will be revealed on spot.
2. Top three teams will be awarded.
