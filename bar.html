BARTER   OFF
"From Emotions to materials, it’s all about buying and selling."

Introduction
In trade, barter is a system of exchange where participants in a transaction directly exchange goods or services for other goods or services without using a medium of exchange, such as money. This competition works almost similar to it. In this a product will be given to you on the day of the competition through a draw. You have to sell the given product to the judges within a time frame of 4 minute.


Eligibility and participation criteria

  1. 1. Participants can participate individually or in a team of two.
  2. 2. Registration will be done online until 19th April - 11:59 pm.
  3. 3. There will be only one round.
  4. 4. This competition is open for all students. (graduates and undergraduates)
  5. 5. Only the Team leader has to register along with the Team members name and Team name.


Judging criteria
Marks will be given according to your-
• Originality in idea.
• Way of presentation
• Need of product in market.
• Demonstrated knowledge of target market.
• The final decision will the decision of judges and coordinators.
• The judges will decide the winner of competition.

Venue - Incubation hub, HBTU KANPUR
Date - 20th April 2019
Results will be announced on 21st April.
