GAME   OF   THRONES


Whats the fuss all about:-
"When you play the game of thrones, you win or you die”

The winter is finally here and whether the bastard rises to the throne or snow dragon freeze Westros’s azz out is outta our control. But what's in our control is to create a mighty kingdom that can withstand all of the ice and fire and this requires a wicked genius. Do you have it in you to build and run a kingdom that has the resources to match the wits of Cersei along with the ferocity of the wildlings. If you answered “yes” to the last question please wait this one out but if you just screamed “Hell yeah!” with song of ice and fire running in the back of your cerebrum, you are eligible to venture into this stupid fantasy of a game.

Description:-
The game includes creating a kingdom that can withstand all attacks, seiges and several other possible scenarios .Participants have to strategise and control their spending on several assets from army to food since the resources are limited(Iron Bank is all out of favors for you). You will have approx 30 to 45 minutes to create the required model. All these strategic decisions from offense to diplomacy have to be decided within these generous constraints of time and currency. When you submit your model kingdom, judges will check it against an algorithm that we designed to determine the one closest to the absolute leader of the seven kingdoms. The model that withstands most or all of our filters will be declared winner. You can review your submissions and ask the judge about your shortcomings after the results.
*Spoiler Alert*
We can’t disclose any more details as you not knowing anything might just win you the throne.

Venue:- Auditorium
Date:- April 20,2019


Rules:-

1. Students can participate in teams with maximum strength of 3 participants
2. The coordinators can play Surprise-Madafaka-card anytime during the competition which allows them to change any or all rules of the game.
3. Knowledge of this hyped series called Game Of Thrones isn’t a requisite at all(whats the fuss about anyways).
4. Using internet is unfortunately not allowed during the event.
5. The details given at the time of event will be the only and exhaustive facts you require to play, (again) no prior knowledge of GOT is required.
6. Team which has one or more participants who can speak Dothraki fluently will be declared winners on the spot.
