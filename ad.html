AD MAD
“Creativity about life, in all aspects, I think, is still the secret of great creative people.” -Leo Burnett

Introduction:
Public awareness plays an important role in increasing voting percentage.Eligible citizens must be encouraged to cast their votes for nation-building.But,how? Making an awareness advertisement is a better idea, isn’t it? So,grab the opportunity to make a creative and original ad to aware people for voting. You can make video, poster and write-ups.

Guidelines:
1. You can participate in teams of 2-5 members and all the team members have to follow the official Instagram page of Ecellhbtu @ecellhbtu.
2. Registration is to be done online till 13 April,2019.
3. All the teams have to make their own advertisement content on the topic mentioned in the introduction.
4. Teams will send their content (posters, write-ups, videos) before 15 April,2019 ;11:59 pm, on the registration@ecellhbtu.in .


Rules:
1.The video should not exceed the time limit of 60 seconds and not less than 30 seconds, or else the team may get disqualified.(for video content)
2. Ideas must be original.Plagiarism will lead to disqualification of the team.
3. Teams have to record a video of their own involving all team members
4. Final decisions will be of the coordinators.
5. The winner’s team ad will be featured in the E-Summit on 21 April,2019.
6. The content will be posted on official Instagram page of @ecellhbtu.
7. Winners will be chosen by Amar Ujala.
   * Winning posters and write-ups will be published in Amar Ujala newspaper.
   * Winning video will be posted on the twitter page of Amar Ujala.
