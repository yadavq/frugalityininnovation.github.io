Innovation and Incubation Centre

Innovation and Incubation Center established in Civil Engineering Department

In July 2015, an Innovation and Incubation Center has been established in the Civil Engineering Department with the support of Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University Lucknow. The Nodal officers of the centre are: 1. Prof. Sunil Kumar 2. Prof. D. Permar.

Innovation and Incubation Center established in Mechanical Engineering Department

An incubation centre, “Reverse Engineering and Rapid Prototyping” is in progress to be established in Mechanical Engineering Department and and Mr. Jitendra Bhaskar is working as coordinator of this incubation centre.

Establishment of student startup Cell

In the first phase we have established the Student Startup cell to carry out the required activities in time and phased manner related to start ups as it is mandatory according to AICTE.

Developing facilities and infrastructure of Incubation Hub

The Plan for developing facilities and infrastructure of Incubation Hub is as following:

  1. Laboratory to transform ideas into real-time engineering objects
  2. Integrated digital environment for Innovation, Integration, Incubation and Implementation
  3. Ideation Accelerator will be established with the generous funding with the support of Funding agencies that would be a soft facility in the domain of embedded systems, platforms, algorithms and IT related ideas.
  4. Entrepreneurship Research and Training Centre will aim to conduct cutting edge research, teaching and training in entrepreneurship in the Indian context by utilizing the facilities of Incubation Hub and the existing intellectual pool in the University. Plan is attached as Enclosure II.
