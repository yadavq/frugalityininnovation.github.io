H A C K A T H O N

Prizes worth ₹ 30,000

"Innovation is seeing what everybody has seen and thinking what nobody has thought."

The world is facing vast problems that needs to be tackled for the development of our country. Hackathon is a unique initiative to identify new and disruptive digital technology innovations as well as work on an industrial problem for solving the challenges faced by our country.

Hackathon will be conducted in two rounds.

Round 1:
1. It is an online round.
2. You will have to choose a problem statement according to your interest and develop a solution for it.
3. Problem statements will be published by 8th April on the website.
4. The participants will have to analyse, innovate and improvise and come up with the website/application along with a brief presentation that will help to tackle a problem.
5. You can also come up with your self innovation that will help in dealing problems existing in the current scenario.
6. The presentation required in the round 1 should include the basic ideas, solutions and screenshots of website/app.
7. The submissions for round 1 will be through a github link ( with their presentation attached)
8. The participants are required to mail their github links by 18th of April on registration@ecellhbtu.in
9. List of shortlisted candidates will be uploaded on the website by 19th April 9:00pm.


Round 2:
Venue: CSE Seminar Hall
Date: 20 April, 2019

1. Shortlisted candidates have to report at the given venue on 20th April .
2. Shortlisted candidates will have to come with their website/app and with a detailed presentation and present before the judging panel.


Guidelines and Rules :
1. The participants can participate in team of maximum 3 members.
2. Any kind of plagiarism will lead to disqualification.
3. The decision of the judges and the event managers will be final and binding.
4. Registrations will be accepted till 18th April - 11:59pm.

Judging criteria
• Originality in idea.
• Innovation
• Way of presentation
