Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) Between Harcourt Butler Technical University, Kanpur and Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur for Incubation

This Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) was signed on this 27th day of January 2018 (Saturday) having the following Scope and Purpose of this MoU:

IIT Kanpur agrees to incubate incubatee/company that will be shortlisted and recommended by HBTU in the SIDBI Innovation and Incubation Centre at IITK. The purpose of incubation unit will be to:

  1. Promote & interact with, and resource technology/expertise from faculty members and research scholars and laboratory infrastructure in the various departments and centers of the Institute.
  2. Help in getting other kinds of techno-managerial expertise required that is not available within the institute.
  3. Incubating novel technology and business ideas into viable commercial products or services.
  4. Promote innovation through research and knowledge incubation.
  5. Provide research and development and Incubation facilities for budding entrepreneurs amongst students, teachers, and others and to provide help related to legal, financial, marketing and other matters to them.
