PIVOT
PIVOT is organized with a vision of creating an opportunity (or step) for various enthusiastic individuals who have passion and motivation to convert their innovation into an entrepreneurial idea beneficial for the whole world. We test and examine your ability to identify your market, product usability and need of the customer.


Event Structure
– PIVOT will be organised in two rounds –

ROUND I – SCREENING
This round comprises of submission of the summary of your idea through the form provided to you. Teams need to submit the abstract of their idea mentioning little details of the same. Elimination will be done on basis of this abstract. Team should be sending their summary in the pdf format mailing them to -registration@ecellhbtu.in with Subject : ‘PIVOT - SUMMARY’. Please ensure that the details are submitted in the single mail only.
Deadline to Submit your forms with all the detailts is 5:00 PM, 19th April
Shortlisted cndidates will be informed by 8:00 PM, 19th April

ROUND II – FINAL SHOWDOWN
  • • Teams qualifying the screening round are provided a Javelin Board on which you can list out different aspects of your StartUp Idea and examine them with your target audience at E-SUMMIT’19(participants of the event). This event will take place on 20th – 21st April at HBTU, Kanpur.
  • • After examining the different aspect of your Start-Up, You have to give presentation of your idea in-front of Judging panel with the favourable outcomes.
  • • The participants will have whole 20th April and till 3 PM on 21st April, and they have to reflect their findings on the Javelin Board.


    • Rules and Guidelines –
    1. 1. The team of upto 5 students can participate in the event.
    2. 2. Team are advised to submit abstract in the prescribed format before the deadline to become eligible for the ROUND I.
    3. 3. All participants are required to be present with their college IDs at the time of participation.
    4. 4. Final Result will be announced by 21st April.

    Note -Participants should follow the guidelines strictly. In case of any dispute, the final decision will be in the hands of E-SUMMIT, HBTU KANPUR.

    Eligibility –
    Students from any college can participate in the competition. Plagiarism is unacceptable at any level.
