B I N D E R

Binder is a networking event aiming to provide students/delegates having a business idea (The CEO’s) the opportunity to connect with those who can help(The CXO’s). The founders who want to add co-founders can briefly describe about their idea and what expertise do they search for in their CXO, similarly the participants who want to contribute in a startup can enlist their area of expertise and during the E Summit 2019, both the CEO and CXO will be provided with time and venue to interact with each other.

GUIDELINES –
• The CEO will have to register the summary of their idea and the skills they need to achieve it like website and android developer, graphic designer, UI designer etc. The CXO will have to register their skills which can contribute to the company.
• Candidates will be required to submit their skills during the registration of the events.
• The CEOs can submit the list of technical skills required from the candidates either online on the website or offline at the day of the event.
• On the day of the event, both can meet and decide.

Rules and Guidelines –
• People from all around the globe can participate.
• All participants are required to be present with their IDs at the time of participation.
• Do ensure that all companies have a proper list of the skills they require before the starting of the event.

Note - Participants should follow the guidelines strictly. In case of any dispute, the final decision will be in the hands of E-SUMMIT, HBTU KANPUR.

ELIGIBILITY
Students from any college can participate in the event.
Candidates should mention only those skills which are required for the benefit of the company.
