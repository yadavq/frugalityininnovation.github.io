Date: 13th September 2018

GIIEC HBTU provided a platform for every individual to pitch their innovative ideas. So, we had organized a "Pitching Competition" which was held on 13th September, 2018.

There was a mentor session of half an hour before the competition in which the TiE-UP mentor had told the students about how to pitch an idea. And the mentor was the judge for the competition.

Students came up with individual ideas or in a team of two. Students were given 90 seconds to pitch their idea in front of the judges, and were evaluated on that basis. Only the idea was presented by participants and in the most concise manner possible.

This competition was a big learning curve for all students.
